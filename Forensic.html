Digital Forensics

Digital forensics involves the recovery and investigation of material found in digital devices. It's crucial in criminal and civil investigations.

Network Forensics

Network forensics focuses on monitoring and analyzing computer network traffic for the purposes of information gathering, legal evidence, or intrusion detection.

Wireshark is a network protocol analyzer that allows you to capture and interactively browse the traffic running on a computer network. It's widely used by network administrators, security professionals, and forensic investigators.

Autopsy is a digital forensic platform that is used by digital investigators to conduct investigations on disk images. It's an open-source tool that provides a graphical interface to analyze disk images and recover digital evidence.

FTK Imager is a forensic imaging tool that helps forensic investigators create forensic images of hard drives and other storage devices. It's part of the Forensic Toolkit (FTK) suite and is commonly used in forensic investigations.

Mobile Forensics

Mobile forensics deals with the recovery of digital evidence from mobile devices. This includes smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices.
